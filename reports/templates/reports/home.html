About

This system follows the NCCS institutional flow for plagiarism checking. Teachers maintain the official comparison repository, while students can perform secure temporary scans.

Teacher Access

  • Upload documents to the permanent repository.
  • Scan documents against stored teacher uploads.
  • View repository reports and scan results.

Student Access

  • Scan documents temporarily without storing them in the database.
  • View the latest personal scan result from the dashboard.
  • Use the same plagiarism comparison engine as teachers.
